What is a Crypto Tax Calculator?

A crypto tax calculator is a software tool designed to help calculate taxes owed on cryptocurrency transactions. These tools take into account the token's value, transaction dates, and other factors to generate a tax report.

How Does a Crypto Tax Calculator Work?

A crypto tax calculator works by:

  • Gathering data on Token 104 transactions, including dates, amounts, and exchange rates
