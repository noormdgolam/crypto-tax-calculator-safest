Understanding Tax Implications

Before diving into the calculator, it's essential to understand the tax implications of Token 29 transactions. Token 29 is considered a security and is subject to capital gains tax. When selling Token 29, you will need to calculate the gain or loss, taking into account the purchase price, sale price, and any fees associated with the transaction.

  • Gain: When the sale price exceeds the purchase price, you incur a capital gain.
  • Loss: When the sale price is less than the purchase price, you incur a capital loss.
  • Fees: Transaction fees, exchange fees, and other costs associated with buying and selling Token 29 will impact your tax liability.
