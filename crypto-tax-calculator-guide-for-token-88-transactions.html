Key Tax Concepts for Token 88 Transactions

When trading or disposing of Token 88, there are several tax concepts to consider:

  • Capital Gains Tax: This tax applies to the profit earned from selling or disposing of Token 88 at a higher price than its original purchase value.
  • Losses: If the sale price of Token 88 is lower than its original purchase value, the difference can be claimed as a loss, reducing taxable income.
  • Fair Market Value (FMV): The value of Token 88 at the time of sale or exchange is essential for calculating capital gains tax.
