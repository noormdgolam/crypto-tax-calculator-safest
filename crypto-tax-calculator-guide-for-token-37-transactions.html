Step 1: Identify Your Token 37 Holdings

Before using a Crypto Tax Calculator, it is essential to gather accurate information about your Token 37 holdings. This includes the initial purchase date, purchase price per unit, and the number of units purchased. You should also have records of any subsequent transactions, such as sales, trades, or airdrops.

Importing Transaction Data

To calculate your tax liability, you will need to import your Token 37 transaction data into the Crypto Tax Calculator. Most calculators allow you to import data from various sources, including:

Configure these settings according to your specific situation to receive accurate tax calculations for your Token 37 transactions.

Understanding Crypto Tax Calculator Outputs

A Crypto Tax Calculator will typically generate various reports and tax estimates based on your input data and settings. Key outputs to pay attention to include:
